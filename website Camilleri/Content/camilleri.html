Cargolux Boeing 747

A Cargolux Boeing 747-400F registered as LX-JCV lining up on runway 06 via intersection B1.




Honeywell 757

A face to face with the Honeywell testbed Boeing 757-200 registered as N757HW. This plane is used by the Honeywell subsiadary known as Honeywell Aero which develops new technology for airplanes. This is the plane they use to test their new technology on. It was here in Luxembourg to demonstrate some new SATCOM technology that they are working on.
