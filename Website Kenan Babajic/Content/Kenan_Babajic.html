Animals of Blue Ridge Parkway

Swallowtail butterflies are large, colorful butterflies in the family Papilionidae, and include over 550 species.

The coyote is a species of canine native to North America. It is smaller than its close relative, the wolf, and slightly smaller than the closely related eastern wolf and red wolf.

The white-tailed deer is a medium-sized deer native to North America, Central America, Ecuador, and South America as far south as Peru and Bolivia.
